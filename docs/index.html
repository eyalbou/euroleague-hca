Is it the referees?
Per-referee home-vs-away call asymmetry on 61 officials with 30+ games. Funnel plot, Holm correction, permutation test. Null result: zero biased refs after correction -- rules out referee bias as a meaningful mechanism.

TRACK B What happens next on the floor?

Two dashboards, each answering one dynamics-level question -- about the flow of possessions, not who wins.

After action X, what comes next?
Pick any action (3-pt make, turnover, block, personal foul...) and see three answers: the literal next event, the opponent's immediate reply, and your team's first action next possession. Heatmap, per-team splits, and Storylines (second-order chains) included.
Play-by-play transitions →
After a miss, who gets the rebound? +FILTERS
OREB vs DREB by miss type: 3-point miss, 2-point miss, terminal missed free throw. 2PT misses are offensive-rebounded 3.8 pp more often than 3PT misses; terminal FT misses only 16.6%. Wilson 95% CIs + pairwise z-tests. New: filter by any team subset and season subset -- all rates and comparisons recompute live.
Rebound rates →
Bridge between the tracks: the transitions dashboard has a dedicated HCA lens view showing per-action Δ PPP between home and away possessions -- that's where Track B partially re-connects to Track A. The headline: 94% of the +3.88 HCA is explained by a tiny (+0.049) per-possession efficiency edge repeated across ~72 possessions. Otherwise, Track B is its own investigation about how basketball flows, independent of who's at home.

What each chart says

Where HCA comes from
  • +3.4 pts per 100 possessions efficiency gap at home
  • +1.6 pp higher free-throw rate at home
  • -1.0 fewer turnovers per 100 possessions
  • +0.9 pp eFG% at home (shooting quality)
  • Pace and offensive rebounding are not meaningfully different
Possession-level HCA
  • Every source action shows home > road on next-possession PPP
  • Top gaps: CMU, AG, FTA, TO, OF
  • Volume-weighted: +0.049 PPP → ~3.6 pts / game
  • 94% of the 3.86-pt LM intercept is "explained" by possession efficiency
Modeling
  • Elo + is_home logistic → 64.5% test accuracy
  • Full logistic (+ attendance + rest + interactions) → 65.7%
  • Mixed-effects LM for per-team slopes; Ridge on team fixed effects
  • All bootstraps clustered at game level
COVID natural experiment
  • Closed-doors 2019-20 / 2020-21 as a natural experiment
  • Difference-in-differences on home_pts - away_pts
  • post − pre = +0.11 pts/game (CIs overlap zero)
  • HCA effectively recovered post-pandemic
