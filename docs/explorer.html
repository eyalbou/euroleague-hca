Does home-court advantage live in certain teams or certain seasons?

The league-level +3.73 HCA (Track A) is an average. This tool lets you slice it. Pick any combination of teams and seasons -- every KPI and chart below recomputes in the browser over the filtered slice. Use it to answer questions the static dashboards can't: is a given team's home edge driven by a particular era? Did a specific season swing the league HCA? Does the COVID dip appear for every team?
